# Monkey Brain

A minimalist browser extension that helps you tame your "monkey brain" by blocking addictive, algorithmic short-form content.

### Features
- **Facebook Reels Blocker:** Detects Facebook Reels and instantly redirects you to your standard Facebook feed to prevent doom-scrolling.
- **Instagram Blocker:** Detects Instagram Reels and the Explore page, instantly redirecting you back to your standard Instagram feed.
- **YouTube Home Redirect:** Routes your YouTube homepage directly to your Subscriptions feed so you aren't distracted by algorithms.
- **Granular Controls:** Choose exactly which feeds you want to block with a sleek, minimalist popup UI.
- **Privacy First:** Only runs on YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram. No trackers, no data collection.
- **SPA Aware:** Respects the modern history APIs of major social sites for a seamless, glitch-free experience across Single Page Apps.
